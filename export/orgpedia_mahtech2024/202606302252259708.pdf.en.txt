2.The said orders shall come into force with immediate effect.
3. The Director, Higher Education may, as may be necessary, issue appropriate orders assigning additional charge of the post of Administrative Officer (University Branch), Directorate of Higher Education, Pune to Mr. Suyash Madhukar Dusane.

4. The Head of the Office concerned shall relieve the transferred officers forthwith to join the place of transfer. Also, the officers concerned should immediately join the office in which they were posted after their transfer from the existing office.
5. The Head of the Office concerned shall immediately depute the transferred officers on their discharge from the office concerned. The head of the office concerned shall be held liable in the event of payment of salary and allowances without compulsory waiting period SOA work due to non-acquisition of officers by the head of the office concerned. Also, no leave of absence shall be granted to such officers during the period from discharge to joining the place of transfer.
The period of assumption of office shall be admissible to the transferred officers as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, non-acceptance of service and Provisions for period of suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981 for joining the place of transfer. Those concerned should note that their absence will be treated as a "non-deceasing day" if they fail to join during the permissible tenure of office.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune should ensure that the next month's (July) salary of the transferred officers is drawn from the transferred office only.
8. The Director of Higher Education, Pune shall submit the performance report on the transfer order to the Government within 15 days.
9. The said Government Order shall be issued by the Government of Maharashtra.
