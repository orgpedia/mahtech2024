Preamble:-
Whereas, the Government of Maharashtra, Higher and Technical Education Department has, inter alia, recognised/approved the “MahaDnyandeep” initiative and its operating framework vide Government Resolution dated 17 April 2025 referred above.
Whereas, for effective governance, coordination and long-term implementation of a State- led digital education ecosystem, it 1s considered necessary and expedient to establish a not-for- profit company under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013, as a company limited by guarantee and not having share capital.
Whereas the proposed name contains the word “State” and includes the identifier ‘“MahaDnyandeep’’, and for the purposes of name reservation and incorporation, it 15 considered necessary to issue an express Government approval/authorisation for the use of such words in the proposed name, and to authorise the concerned authorities to undertake all actions for incorporation and allied filings.
Government Resolution:-
1) Approval for establishment:
The Government of Maharashtra hereby accords approval to establish a Section 8 Company (Company Limited by Guarantee and Not Having Share Capital) in the name and style of:
“MAHARASHTRA STATE ONLINE EDUCATION — MAHADNYANDEEP” or such other name as may be approved by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs / Registrar of Companies / Central Registration Centre for the purposes of incorporation, provided that the approved name shall retain the identifier “MahaDnyandeep” and/or the word “State’’, as applicable
# Page 2
2) Permission to use “State” and “MahaDnyandeep” in the name:
For the limited purposes of (1) name reservation and (1) incorporation under the Companies Act, 2013, the Government of Maharashtra hereby expressly permits and authorises the Proposed
Company to use:
(a) the word “State” as part of its name to reflect that it 15 a State-led initiative; and
(b) the word “MahaDnyandeep” as part of its name, being the identifier of the Government- recognised “MahaDnyandeep” initiative.
3) Authorisation to undertake all further steps:
The Government of Maharashtra hereby authorises:
(1) Higher and Technical Education Department (HTED), Government of Maharashtra; and
(11) Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University (YCMOU), Nashik,
to undertake all necessary actions for establishment and incorporation of the Proposed Company, including but not limited to: applying for name reservation; signing, submitting and
filing requisite forms, declarations and documents with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs /
Registrar of Companies / Central Registration Centre; completing DIN/DSC and other statutory prerequisites; finalising and executing incorporation documents; applying for and obtaining Section 8 licence; and completing other allied registrations/filings incidental to incorporation.
4) Continuation with earlier Government Resolution:
This Government Resolution 15 issued in continuation of the Government Resolution dated 17
April 2025 and shall be read harmoniously with it.
5) Submission to ROC/CRC:
A certified copy of this Government Resolution may be furnished to the Registrar of Companies / Central Registration Centre as supporting document for name reservation and incorporation of the Proposed Company.
This Government Resolution shall be made available on the website www.maharashtra.gov.in
