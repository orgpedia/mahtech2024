The Proposal: -
Department of Higher and Technical Education. The Governing Body was established at Sri Guru Gobind Singh Ji Institute of Engineering and Technology (SGGS), Vishnupuri, Nanded by a Government Decision dated 17.01.2023. The term of the Governing Body mentioned in the said Government Decision is for a period of 3 years and the term of office of the persons nominated by the University Grants Commission is for a period of 5 years. Accordingly, the term of office of the Government-appointed members of the Governing Board of the Institute has expired on the date 17.01.2026.
Accordingly, the Government was contemplating the re-constitution of the Governing Body of Sri Guru Gobind Singh Ji Institute of Engineering and Technology, Nanded, a non-aided autonomous institution.
The Government's Decisions:

The Chairman and Members of the Board of Management shall be entitled to travel concessions, immigration allowance and meeting allowance payable to an officer of the level of Secretary of the State of Maharashtra for the meetings of the Board of Regulators constituted under this Government Decision and for other business connected with the Board.
