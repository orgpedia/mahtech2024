The Government's Decisions:
Kavikulguru Kalidasa Sanskrit University, Ramtek Act, 1997 and Reference No. 2 As per the procedure prescribed by the Government Decision, Kavikulguru Kalidasa Sanskrit Vishwavidyalaya, Ramtek, Nagpur, a university in the state has recommended to the Government for approval of proposals for new colleges for the academic year 2026-27. The proposals were scrutinized as per the norms laid down by the relevant Government Decision. Proposals qualified as per Government Scrutiny at Government level have been approved by Kavikulguru Kalidasa Sanskrit Vishwavidyalaya, Ramtek Act, 1997 under Section 62 (5) of the Act, as per the powers vested in the Government and Reference No. 3 A total of 2 new colleges mentioned in the accompanying prospectus are being sanctioned on a perpetual non-subsidized basis subject to the following terms and conditions, within the revised timelines as prescribed by the Ordinance herein.
Terms and Conditions: - 1. The affiliation of the colleges in the attached statement shall be on a perpetual non-aided basis as per the prevailing policy of the Government. Also, the government will not be responsible for the salary and non-payment of the teaching and non-teaching staff of these colleges.
