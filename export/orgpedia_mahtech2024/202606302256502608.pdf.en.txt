2.The said orders shall come into force with immediate effect.
3. The Principal / Director concerned shall immediately relieve the transferred faculty to join the place of transfer. Also, the faculty concerned should immediately transfer from the existing college / institution to the college / institution in which they are posted.
4. The Principal / Director concerned shall appoint the transferred faculty without delay on their retirement from the concerned college / institution. The principal / director concerned is liable for non-acquisition of teachers who have come to join.
# Page 3
In the event of payment of salary and allowances without SOA working during the waiting period, the Principal / Director concerned will be held responsible. Also, no leave of absence shall be granted to such teachers during the period from discharge to joining the place of transfer.
The period of assumption of office shall be admissible to the transferred faculty as per the provisions of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption of office, non-acceptance of service and Provisions for period of suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981, as per the extant rules of the Government, for joining the place of transfer. Those concerned should note that their period of absence will count as a "non-deceasing day" if they do not join during the permissible tenure of office.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune should ensure that the salaries of the transferred teachers for the next month (for the month of July) are drawn from the transferred college itself.
The Director of Higher Education, Pune, shall submit the performance report on the transfer order to the Government within 15 days.
8. The said Government Order shall be issued by the Government of Maharashtra.
